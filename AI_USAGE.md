# AI usage report

During this project, we used several AI tools such as such ChatGPT or Gemini integrated into Google. For more complex tasks, we used Сodex in VS Code IDE. Specifically, for assessing the code structure and integrating AI assistant.

AI was used as an assistant throughout the whole development process. It helped us in several tasks. For example, AI bots suggested initial code (stucture), debug errors as well as understand issues. It helped us to break down complex tasks into smaller steps and suggested approaches needed for our pairwork.

AI-generated outputs were carefully reviewed and was understood before being used. More often than not, generated code required modification, or was fully rewritten. We often asked follow-up questions to better understand the suggested logic/solutions and modified this code to fit our needs. We also tested all implementations manually to ensure correctness and compatibility with the rest of the system.

AI-generated code could have been sometimes inefficient, overly complex or just not aligned with our needs. In other words, relying on AI without proper understanding would have caused errors or poor design/structure decisions.

The final application was not produced automatically by AI. We were responsible for designing the structure, integrating components, debugging and refining. AI acted as an assistant rather than a standalone coder or solution provider. Thus, all final decisions and implementations were made by us.
